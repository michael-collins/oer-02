The Bauhaus (example content)

Description

The Bauhaus was an elite school in Germany that was active between 1919 to 1933. The school, based in Berlin starting in 1932, was shuttered after a run-in with Hitler's Gestapo. Fields of study in art and design were integrated, fostering an environment that produced work and ideas that have profoundly influenced contemporary artist and designers.
